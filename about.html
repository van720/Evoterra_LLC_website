Trevor Carter

PRINCIPAL/MANAGER

Trevor, founder of Evoterra, brings over 16 years of senior-level experience in multifamily development and construction management. A BYU Construction Management graduate, he has developed over 275 units and built and transacted over $215 million in multifamily and commercial real estate transactions while overseeing land acquisition, site development, and project stabilization.

Trevor is intimately involved in land purchases, zoning, entitlements, permitting, site development, architectural design, construction budgeting, construction financing, construction management, and stabilization — developing and building more than half a million square feet of construction projects.

Trevor currently serves on Anthem's Growth Committee and on the Board of Directors for Anthem's Parkside community of 7,229 homes for nearly 20,000 residents.

Alex Jenkins

VP of Construction

Alex, Vice President of Construction at Evoterra, oversees all construction operations, ensuring excellence from start to finish. With over a decade of experience, he specializes in managing complex projects and optimizing processes. Alex's background spans specialty government contracting, the renewables sector, and multifamily development.
